Barcelona: A Place Worth Visiting

One of my dream destinations has always been Barcelona, Spain.
Barcelona has so much to offer, that any type of traveller young or old, will find a plethora of things to see and do during their vacation.

Jaw-Dropping Architecture

"The most spectacular – and controversial – of Antoni Gaudí’s modernist masterpieces, the Sagrada Família has been under construction for over 100 years and is not expected to be completed for another 20 years. Rich in religious symbolism intertwined with themes of nature and unusual geometric forms, this is one of the most visually intriguing monuments you’re likely to find in Europe."
— 11 Reasons Why You Should Visit Barcelona at Least Once in Your Lifetime

A City Break and Beach Holiday all in one

Whether you're looking for a busy city break or to be within a stones throw from beautiful beaches, Barcelona is certainly a destination that should be on your bucket list for 2020.
